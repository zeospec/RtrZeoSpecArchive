Rtr.Rtn. Arun Teja Godavarthi

PP Rtr. Rtn. Arun Teja Godavarthi, started his Rotaract Journey as a Charter Member of Rotaract Club of CMRIT in RIY 2015 – 2016. He is now a Charter member of Rotaract Bangalore East & Rotarian from Rotary Bangalore East.

Rtr. Arun has led the leadership efforts of Rotaract District Council 3190 in various capacities, including 2 consecutive tenures in the District Secretariat. He will be servicing as the Charter District Rotaract Representative for RI District 3191 in RIY 2023 – 2024

Rtr. Arun started his professional journey as a Founding member at the mere age of 16. He continued his Entrepreneurial journey across numerous ventures. Continuing his successful journey he transitioned as a Community Strategy Consultant, consulting for over 40+ clients across the Globe. Currently, Arun works as a Community Manager managing the Google's Developers Communities in India.
