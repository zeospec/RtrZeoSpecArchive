Why PREFACE?

PREFACE - "Connect with DRR Elect" is an effort and an opportunity for the Rotaract Clubs and the District Rotaract Representative Elect to exchange views, opinions, concerns & ideas to build a better understanding and bonding, as we shape the new beginnings of RI District 3191.

Expectations

Opportunities

Get to Know

An opportunity for the Club & the DRR Elect to understand each other’s aims, objectives, and goals towards the Rotaract Movement.

Guide for PREFACE

To have a fun-filled interactive session along with efficiently exchange the ideologies with the District Rotaract Representative Elect heed the following steps -

Confirm the Slot

Based on the availability of the members & the club’s meeting schedule, book and confirm the slot.

Inform your Club

Inform all the members, the Board of Directors & President Elects / Secretary Designates to be part of PREFACE to connect and voice their feedback, opinions, concerns & ideas as we shape the new beginnings of RI District 3191.

Reminders about the PREFACE

Send timely reminders to enable maximum participation.

Casual & Fun

Feedback & Next Steps

Circulate the feedback forms to gather further thoughts, questions & suggestions.
